Warranty & Return Policy

WARRANTY

Engines - These complete assemblies including manifolds, oil pan, timing belts, and covers, fuel injection or carburetor, we guarantee only the long block. Accessories such as but not limited to Turbos, Starters, air compressors, alternator, power steering pumps, optical distributors, or electric water pumps. If these parts are purchased separately they will be under warranty Other items that may need to be replaced are the water pump, distributor cap, spark plugs, and wires, and possibly timing chain. Oscar Garage Auto will not be held liable for these parts listed as they are considered high-wear parts.

Oscar Garage Auto will not return or refund any units until the customer can provide written documentation from an Authorized Dealer proving the part was reprogrammed and still not functioning properly. All electrical items have a replacement warranty only Up to one replacement will be sent in order to rectify a non-working module. Modules may need to be reset by the dealer.Bushings and Ball Joints are not guaranteed on parts


Transmission - We guarantee the transmission shifts well, the gears, and the bearings to be good. Doors are complete assemblies however they are not guaranteed to come with such accessories as windows, window regulators, handles, hinges, or interior panels. We only guarantee the door shell.We do not promise any specific color. Body parts that do not match the color of your vehicle will not be available for return for that reason. We recommend repainting the part to the vehicle color

RETURN POLICY

We always make sure to reach the customer's satisfaction. IF IN CASE the customer receives an incorrect, damaged, or defective part, within 7 business days the customer can notify the customer service with proof (picture, in writing) within 3 days We can replace the part or refund the money back. Do Not send the part back before receiving a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA).
